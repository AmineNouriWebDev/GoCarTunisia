About Us

Presentation of our agency "GoCarTunisia"

GoCarTunisia is a Tunisian family-owned car rental company since Febrary 2023 with a capital of one hundred thousand dinars (200.000 Tunisian Dinar). The head office is located at Avenue Moncef Bey 8050 Hammamet, Yasmine Hammamet road.

GoCarTunisia is committed to providing serious and professional service to its clients (individuals, tourists, and professionals), with recent and well-maintained vehicles available in various types, models, and categories. Experienced GoCarTunisia agents deliver the car to the agreed location and time, while keeping prices reasonable and competitive.

Today, we lead the top tier of independent Tunisian car rental agencies. The GoCarTunisia agency is present at all Tunisian airports including Tunis Carthage Airport, Enfidha Airport, Monastir Airport, Sfax Airport, and Djerba Airport, as well as in all Tunisian cities including Tunis, Gammarth, La Marsa, Bizerte, Hammamet, Nabeul, Enfidha, Sousse, Monastir, Sfax, Djerba…

We offer car rentals for short or long durations, and provide dedicated services to businesses where we handle fleet management, maintenance, and insurance.

Thanks to our "GoCarTunisia at Home" service, the car is delivered to the client’s home, hotel, or place of residence.

"Our job is always at your service!" We provide personalized advice to each of our clients. We listen to them to offer the best solution tailored to their needs. We distinguish ourselves from our competitors through our innovative products, personalized welcome, service quality, seriousness, and unmatched delivery.

Our Values 

Proximity

We build a relationship of trust and proximity with our clients. Customer Relationship is at the heart of our approach: "All our collaborators and agents love their clients; they consider them as partners."

Customer Satisfaction

We are available to our clients 24/7 and can provide a vehicle for every need, from one to a hundred vehicles for a client for a specified period. "There is a steering wheel for every client."

Innovation

We stand out from our competitors with our innovative ideas, with a strong desire to make constant and daily improvements. We always strive to offer innovative products. In recent years, we have launched luxury car rentals with a chauffeur, long-term rentals dedicated to businesses (LLD), and airport transfer and shuttle services.

Team Spirit

Our activity has tripled in size in one year rapidly thanks to our dedicated team. Our team is very attached to the "GoCarTunisia" brand and considers themselves as their own boss. Each person performs their task with maximum respect and seriousness.

Solidarity

GoCarTunisia is a human and family-oriented company. In this sense, we support several charitable associations and are partners with some sports teams to demonstrate our commitment in various Tunisian regions.

Your road is our passion! 😊
